frequently Asked questions

How do I buy a game?

It's super-easy. First, you need to add the game to your cart. You can do it in two places:

  • the Browse page, by clicking on the 'Add to Cart' button, or
  • the product page (like this one), by clicking on the 'Add to Cart' button.

Once you've got one or more items in your Cart, click on the Cart icon in the upper-right corner of the screen, and select "Proceed to Checkout".

Once in Checkout, you will have to enter your billing address and card details and, after all data is entered, click on "Pay Now" button.

I cannot log in. What can I do?

If you've already tried resetting your password and are still having trouble accessing your account, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at info@gamebox.com.

How do I reset my password?

If you happen to forget your password, simply go to our "Sign In" page and click on the "Forgotten Password" link. From there, you'll need to enter your email address and click on the "Reset My Password" button. We will then email you a reset link that you can use to create a new password.

How can I change my currency?

Currently, our games are only offered in GBP. However, we are working on making them available in other currencies as well. Once this feature is implemented, we will send an email to all of our account holders to let them know. So, if you haven't already signed up, it might be a good idea to do so! (Hint, hint.)

How can I change my currency?

If you're not currently logged in, there's no need to worry about providing your billing address until you decide to make a purchase. At that point, we'll request the necessary information from you.

However, if you're logged in and want to change your default billing address, simply go to "My Profile" in the navigation bar. From there, you can update your billing address and save it as your new default by checking the "Save as default billing address" box. If you'd like to use a different billing address for a specific purchase, you can leave that box unchecked and enter the new details during checkout.

How do I download my purchased items?

After you've completed your purchase, we'll send an email to your inbox with a detailed description of your order and a unique serial key for each item you've purchased. To redeem your game, simply copy the serial key and follow the link provided in the email to our partner's website.

On our partner's website, you'll be prompted to enter the serial key and verify your account information. Once you've completed these steps, your new game will be added to your account and you'll be able to start playing right away.

We understand that this redemption process may seem unusual, but we assure you that it's a reliable and secure way to ensure that you receive your game as quickly as possible. If you have any questions or concerns about the process, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team for assistance.
