Who are we? What do we do?

Welcome to GAMEBOX, where gaming is serious business...and by serious, we mean seriously fun! So, who are we? Well, we sell video games. Duh. But also...

Our Story

Once upon a time, in a tiny basement apartment (with questionable lighting), a group of avid gamers came together to create GAMEBOX. Fueled by their passion for gaming, they embarked on a quest to bring the latest and greatest video games to fellow gamers around the world.

Our Mission

At GAMEBOX, our mission is simple: to level up your gaming experience. We're on a mission to provide the most epic games and the most immersive gameplay, all while delivering top-notch customer service. We want to make sure that every time you play a game from GAMEBOX, you're transported to another world, where your only concern is mastering your gaming skills.

Our Values

We're more than just a bunch of button mashers. At GAMEBOX, we believe in inclusivity and equality. We proudly support LGBTQI+ charities with a percentage of our profits, because we believe that gaming should be a safe and welcoming space for everyone. We're also committed to environmental sustainability, and we're constantly exploring ways to reduce our carbon footprint and promote eco-friendly practices.

But enough about us, let's talk about you! As a GAMEBOX customer, you're not just a player, you're part of our gaming community. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we're always here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

So, gear up, grab your controller, and let's embark on an epic gaming adventure together!
